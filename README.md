# Our Calm House — to share with friends & family

A little look at the calm, healthy home we're dreaming up, comparing **Nuvali** and **Baguio**.

- **[index.html](index.html)** — landing page, with a 🌙 day/night toggle.
- **[Calm-House-Checklist.html](Calm-House-Checklist.html)** — a research-backed healthy-home checklist (light, movement, sleep, clean air & water, nourishment, community), with notes for Nuvali vs Baguio and links to real lots.
- **[House-3D.html](House-3D.html)** — an interactive 3D model of a 5-bedroom, open-plan one-storey home. Rotate it, move the sun through the day, try the weather (rain, typhoon, fog…) and even an earthquake, and switch night mode.

Anchored to a real lot in each place (sizes shown; no prices):

| | Lot | Size |
|---|---|---|
| Baguio | Rolling Hills, San Luis Village | 432 m² |
| Nuvali | Treveia (Alveo · Ayala Land) | 297 m² |
